About Griffins

GRM Griffins Robert Maina

A Leader for Kitutu Chache North

Griffins Robert Maina is a distinguished Kenyan Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with a proven track record in financial management, audit, and economic policy. His journey from the communities of Kitutu Chache North to professional excellence has given him deep insight into the needs and aspirations of his constituents.

As an aspiring Member of Parliament for Kitutu Chache North Constituency, Griffins is committed to bringing transformative leadership that addresses the unique challenges facing the region while leveraging its immense potential for growth and development.

Our Vision

"To transform Kitutu Chache North into a model constituency where every resident has access to quality education, healthcare, economic opportunities, and sustainable development. I believe in leadership that is transparent, accountable, and focused on delivering tangible results for the people we serve."

— Griffins Robert Maina

Priority Areas

📚

Education

Improving access to quality education for all children in Kitutu Chache North. Investing in schools, teacher training, and educational infrastructure to create opportunities for the next generation.

🏥

Healthcare

Enhancing healthcare facilities and services to ensure every resident has access to affordable, quality healthcare. Focus on preventive care and medical infrastructure.

💼

Economic Development

Creating jobs and economic opportunities through support for local businesses, entrepreneurship programs, and attracting investment to the constituency.

🌾

Agriculture

Supporting farmers with modern farming techniques, inputs, and market access. Transforming agriculture into a viable and sustainable livelihood for local communities.

🛣️

Infrastructure

Improving roads, water supply, and other essential infrastructure to connect communities and enable economic growth throughout the constituency.

🤝

Youth & Women Empowerment

Creating programs that empower youth and women through skills training, mentorship, and access to capital for business and agricultural ventures.
