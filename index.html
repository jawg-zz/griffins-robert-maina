About Griffins

GRM Griffins Robert Maina

A Leader for Kitutu Chache North

Griffins Robert Maina is a distinguished Kenyan People's Champion with a proven track record in financial management, audit, and economic policy. His journey from the communities of Kitutu Chache North to professional excellence has given him deep insight into the needs and aspirations of his constituents.

As an aspiring Member of Parliament for Kitutu Chache North Constituency, Griffins is committed to bringing transformative leadership that addresses the unique challenges facing the region while leveraging its immense potential for growth and development.

Our Policy Agenda

Comprehensive solutions for Kitutu Chache North

📚

Education Transformation

Key Initiatives:

  • Renovate and equip all 45 primary schools with modern facilities
  • Establish 5 new digital learning centers in remote areas
  • Provide 2,000 annual bursaries for secondary and tertiary education
  • Train 150 teachers annually on modern pedagogical methods
  • Create 3 vocational training centers for skills development
  • Establish a constituency education fund of KES 50 million annually
Impact: 95% transition rate from primary to secondary, 80% digital literacy among students
🏥

Healthcare Access

Key Initiatives:

  • Upgrade Marani Sub-County Hospital to Level 4 status
  • Equip all 7 ward health centers with essential medicines
  • Employ 50 community health workers for home-based care
  • Establish mobile clinic services for remote areas
  • Create a health insurance scheme for 5,000 vulnerable families
  • Construct 3 new dispensaries in underserved areas
Impact: Universal health coverage for 90% of residents, reduced maternal mortality by 40%
🛣️

Infrastructure Development

Key Initiatives:

  • Tarmac 45km of key roads connecting major trading centers
  • Gravel and maintain 120km of rural access roads
  • Install solar street lighting in all 7 ward headquarters
  • Construct 15 modern market sheds for traders
  • Drill 25 boreholes for clean water access
  • Extend electricity to 3,000 new households
Impact: 95% road accessibility, 100% access to clean water, 85% electricity coverage
🌾

Agricultural Revolution

Key Initiatives:

  • Establish 5 fertilizer and seed subsidy programs
  • Create 3 modern produce collection centers
  • Install 10 solar-powered irrigation systems
  • Train 2,000 farmers on modern agricultural techniques
  • Establish 7 cooperative societies for collective marketing
  • Introduce crop insurance for 1,500 farmers
Impact: 40% increase in farm income, 60% reduction in post-harvest losses
💼

Youth & Women Empowerment

Key Initiatives:

  • Establish a KES 20 million youth enterprise fund
  • Create 500 direct jobs through constituency projects
  • Train 1,000 youth in digital skills and entrepreneurship
  • Support 200 women-led businesses with startup capital
  • Establish 3 business incubation centers
  • Provide business development services to 500 entrepreneurs
Impact: 70% youth employment rate, 300 new businesses created annually
🤝

Social Protection

Key Initiatives:

  • Expand cash transfer programs to 1,000 vulnerable households
  • Establish 3 rescue centers for gender-based violence victims
  • Create special education programs for 200 children with disabilities
  • Provide assistive devices to 150 persons with disabilities
  • Establish feeding programs in 20 primary schools
  • Create elderly care programs for 300 senior citizens
Impact: 100% coverage of vulnerable populations, reduced poverty rate by 30%
